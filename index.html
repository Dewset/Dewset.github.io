    Biography¶

    I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Soochow University, where I am a member of Soochow NLP Lab, majored in software engineering. I am advised by Prof. Qiaoming Zhu and Prof. Peifeng Li. Before that, I obtained the bachelor degree from China University of Petroleum (East China) , QinDao Institute Of Software, College Of Computer Science And Technology in 2022.
